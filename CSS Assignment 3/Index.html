Snickers: The Bar of Joy

Introduction

Snickers is a brand name chocolate bar made by the American company Mars, Incorporated, consisting of nougat topped with caramel and peanuts that has been enrobed in milk chocolate.

Interesting Facts

  • Snickers is the best selling candy bar of all time and its annual global sales exceed $2 billion.
  • The Snickers candy bar was named after a horse that the Mars family owned.
  • There are also several other Snickers products such as Snickers mini, dark chocolate, ice cream bars, Snickers with almonds, Snickers peanut butter bars, Snickers with Extra Caramel and Snickers with Extra Nougat.

Nutritional Information

Each bar contains 250 calories, 12 grams of fat, 4.5 grams of saturated fat, 27 grams of carbohydrates, 1 gram of fiber, 22 grams of sugar, and 4 grams of protein.
